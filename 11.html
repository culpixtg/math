1v1 Soccer

1v1 Soccer is a cartoony soccer game where players score points and compete against an enemy to beat an opponent. Players can choose either to compete against another player on the same computer or against an AI opponent. Additionally, players can choose which team they play as as well as which team they play against. The teams consist of Australia, Italy, Portugal, Brazil, England, France, Germany, and Holland. Each team also has many different characters which can be chosen such as Figo, Carvalho, Kewell, and Cahill. All of the different characters have different stats that allow the player to choose their playstyle that they use to score more goals and win.
